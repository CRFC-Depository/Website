Online Giving

If you would like to offer a tithe or make a special offering, by far the fastest, easiest, and most secure way to do so is to click the button below or the Online Giving button from the website menu. Contributions are always confidential, and no one at the church sees your private information.

  • You can use a credit or debit card, or your own PayPal account.
  • You can designate your gift to general tithes, missions, the building fund, or another project.
  • You can set up a recurring payment, and change it at any time.
  • You can also still mail a check to 555 Commons Drive, St. Helens, OR 97051.
